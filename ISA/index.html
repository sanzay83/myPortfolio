A Message from President of International Student Association

On Behalf of the board of International Student Association it is my pleasure to say welcome Mavericks. My fellow officers, the Executive board, All the Recognized Student Organization presidents and I hope to show how ISA can make difference in your academic and professional experiences every day. ISA encourages and facilitates connections across cultures and provides opportunities for the campus and community to participate in engaging social and cultural events.

I encourage new and returning students to stay connected and up-to-date regarding University and community activities through Engage, our Facebook site and via email.

Additionally, I would like to request you to practice our University’s commitment to equality, civility, and diversity. It is our expectation that all members of the University and local community will behave in a respectful manner and treat others with courtesy and mutual respect.

This will be an exciting academic year, remember Horns Up! and always aim high and continue to reach the goals you all set. I wish each of you a successful academic year.

Sincerely,
Sanjaya Balami
President,
International Student Association.
